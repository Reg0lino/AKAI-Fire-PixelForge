BEYOND THE DEFAULTS: WHAT PIXELFORGE OFFERS

Free your Akai Fire from mundane standby lights. PixelForge gives you full command over every light and the screen.

Paint static designs, craft intricate pad animations, turn your music into a light show, transform your desktop into an ambient visualizer, design custom OLED art, and battle demons in LazyDOOM! It's your controller, supercharged.

DIVE INTO THE FEATURES: TOTAL CREATIVE CONTROL

🎵 NEW! SEE YOUR SOUND: ADVANCED AUDIO VISUALIZER

Turn your Akai Fire into a real-time audio-reactive light show! The new visualizer listens to your system audio and drives the pads with vibrant animations.

Choose from three modes: Classic Spectrum Bars (a graphic EQ), Pulse Wave (a sweeping bar of light), or the pro-level Dual VU + Spectrum. All modes have deep customization options for colors, sensitivity, and smoothness that can be tweaked live!

🎨 THE PADS ARE YOUR CANVAS: PAINT & ANIMATE

Take direct control of all 64 RGB pads. The new Primary/Secondary color picker allows for a professional workflow: left-click to paint with your main color, right-click to paint with your secondary color (or erase).

Then, bring your ideas to life with the Animator Studio: a visual timeline for crafting frame-by-frame animations. Add, delete, duplicate, copy/paste frames with ease, and manage all your animation sequences with built-in file management.

🖥️ MIRROR YOUR WORLD: AMBIENT SCREEN SAMPLING

Turn your pads into a dynamic, real-time reflection of your desktop! Select any region on any connected monitor, and watch as PixelForge translates the on-screen colors to your controller.

Fine-tune the output with live adjustments for Saturation, Contrast, Brightness, and Hue. Want to capture that awesome visual moment? You can record the sampler's output directly into new animation sequences!

✨ OLED WIZARDRY: CUSTOM TEXT, IMAGES & DITHERED GIFS

Transform the 128x64 monochrome screen into your personal message board or micro-cinema! The OLED Content Library Manager lets you create, edit, and save "Text Items" and "Image/Animation Items."

Import static images or animated GIFs and harness the advanced processing pipeline:

  • Pre-Dithering: Adjust Brightness, Contrast, Gamma, Sharpen, and apply Pre-Dither Blur.
  • Dithering Arsenal: Choose from Floyd-Steinberg, Atkinson, Simple Threshold, and multiple Ordered Dithers.
  • Fine Control: Utilize Variable Dither Strength and add stylistic Noise Injection.

👹 LAZYDOOM: RETRO FPS ON YOUR CONTROLLER!
